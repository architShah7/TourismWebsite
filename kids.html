Kids Favorites

Balvatika Amusement Park

Balvatika Amusement Park is similar to Canada's Wonderland's Kids Ville. It consists of a lot of amusement rides for children. There is also a zoo, small doll museum, and many other types of attractions for kids. It is open from 9:00am to 10:15pm all week long.

Maniar Wonderland Snow Park

Maniar's Wonderland consists of a snow park that allows children to experience snow. This is typically enjoyable for childern who love snow and are used to expereincing tropical weather all year long. This park consists of 15+ rides for children. It is open from 10:00am - 7:00pm on Monday-Friday and 10:00am - 8:00pm on the weekend.

Shanku's Water Park

This waterpark is filled with a bunch of water rides which are full of thrill and adventure. Children find this place enjoyable. It consists of Lazy River, Zip Zap zoom, and Aqua Shuttle. It is open everyday from 10:00am to 8:30pm with entry fees up to 500 rupees.
