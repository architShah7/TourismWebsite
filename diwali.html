Diwali

Diwali, also known as “festival of lights”, is a Hindu festival which is celebrated in the month of Kartika (according to the Hindu calendar). It is celebrated over 5 days. People light up various kinds of fireworks and eat a lot of sweets. It marks the beginning of a new year for Hindus. Diwali symbolizes the victory of good over evil.
