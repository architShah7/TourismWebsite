Ahmedabad is home to all kinds of foods!

Paav Bhaji

Paav Bhaji is a mashed vegetable curry served with soft bread, raw onions, and a slice of lime. Vegetables may include tomatoes, potatoes, onions, chillies and peas. Typically, it is garnished with coriander leaves.

Where to find?

Honest Restaurant

Sev Puri

Sevpuri is a Indian chaat dish that is made with crispy whole wheat crackers that are topped with a mashed potato, thin gram flour noodles, onions, and cilantro and tamarind chutneys. It is a sweet and spicy dish.

Where to find?

Diwan Bhel Pakori Centre

Paneer Tikka Masala

Paneer tikka masala is an Indian dish which consists of paneer cheese in a gravy made from a mixture of garlic, cream, butter, onions, tomatoes, cilantro, and traditional Indian spices. It is an extremely popular dish amongst vegetarians. It is typically served with tandoori naan.

Where to find?

Mirch Masala

Butter Chicken

Butter chicken is an Indian dish that consists of marinated chicken in a gravy made from a mixture of garlic, cream, butter, onions, tomatoes, cilantro, and traditional Indian spices. It is highly popular amongst tourists.

Where to find?

Masty Fast Foods Centre
