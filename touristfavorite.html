Tourist Favorites

Kankaria Lake

  • Fees: ₹25 per person for Adults, ₹10 per person for Children
  • Timings: Monday - Closed, Tuesday - Sunday 4:00am - 8:00am, 9:00am - 10:00pm
  • Things to do:
    • Ride the train that takes you around the lake
    • Eat lots of tasty food from various food stalls
    • Hot air balloon ride
    • Stroll around the lake

Jhulta Minar

  • Fees: None
  • Timings: Monday - Sunday 5:30am - 9:00pm
  • Things to do:
    • Observe the mindboggling architecture
    • Take lots of pictures
    • Experience 500-year-old architecture
  • Fun Fact: Scientists do not know the physics behind shaking of only one of the two minarets each time there's an energy transfer.

Adalaj Stepwell

  • Fees: None
  • Timings: Monday - Sunday 6:00am - 6:00pm
  • Things to do:
    • Observe the historical colossal architecture
    • Take lots of pictures
    • Learn about historical significance of the well
