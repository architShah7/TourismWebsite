Uttarayan

Uttarayan, also known as “Festival of Kites” is a Hindu festival that is celebrated on 14/15 January. This festival gets everyone excited about flying kites. People often look forward to kite fighting, in which they get involved in mini battles and cut the string of other kites. The sky is filled with thousands of kites on 14th January.
